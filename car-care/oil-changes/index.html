Meet Tom Langlands

I've always had a real passion for cars. Whether it's keeping them looking their best, carrying out repairs or simply making sure they're safe and reliable, I genuinely enjoy looking after them properly.

As a family man, I know how important a dependable vehicle is. Your car isn't just something you drive, it's what gets you to work, takes the kids to school and helps you make the most of your time with family and friends. That's why I treat every vehicle with the same care and attention I would my own.

Alongside car care, I'm also a professional automatic driving instructor. I enjoy helping people build confidence behind the wheel and seeing learners progress into safe, capable drivers. My aim is always to create a relaxed environment where you can learn at your own pace without feeling under pressure.

Whether you've come to me for a vehicle detail, a repair, servicing or driving lessons, you can expect honest advice, quality workmanship and a friendly, professional service from start to finish.
